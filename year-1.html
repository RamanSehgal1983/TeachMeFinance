Year One: The Foundational Bedrock

The first year of a BBA program is meticulously designed to immerse students in the fundamental "language of business." This initial year is an integrated architectural design intended to cultivate a holistic understanding of the business ecosystem, which is a critical prerequisite for advanced study.
