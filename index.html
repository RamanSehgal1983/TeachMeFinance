Your Journey Through a BBA in Finance

The BBA Finance degree is a structured, three-year academic journey designed to build a well-rounded business professional with deep financial expertise, following a "generalist-to-specialist" path.

Explore the Curriculum
YEAR ONE

Laying the Foundation: The Language of Business

The first year is dedicated to building a solid foundation, providing the fundamental concepts and vocabulary that are prerequisites for all subsequent business and finance studies.

Principles of Management

Introduces the core functions of planning, organizing, leading, and controlling. Covers management theories and organizational structure.

Financial Accounting

Teaches the systematic process of recording, classifying, and summarizing financial transactions to prepare financial statements.

Business Communication

Develops effective written, oral, and non-verbal communication skills essential for a professional business environment.

Business Law

Introduces the legal framework governing business, focusing on the Indian Contract Act and other key commercial laws.

Business Mathematics

Applies mathematical concepts like calculus and matrices to solve business problems and support data-driven decisions.

Microeconomics

Studies economic behavior at the individual and firm level, covering demand, supply, market structures, and consumer behavior.

YEAR TWO

Building Functional Expertise and Financial Depth

The second year cultivates a cross-functional mindset, demonstrating how financial decisions are deeply intertwined with a company's marketing, HR, and overall economic environment.

Financial Management

The primary bridge to finance specialization, introducing time value of money, capital budgeting, risk and return, and working capital management.

Marketing Management

Covers principles and strategies of marketing, including market research, consumer behavior, product strategy, pricing, and promotion.

Human Resource Management

Focuses on managing an organization's workforce, including recruitment, training, performance appraisal, and compensation.

Organizational Behaviour

Delves into the psychology of the workplace, exploring how individual and group dynamics impact organizational effectiveness.

Macroeconomics

Analyzes the economy as a whole, covering national income, inflation, unemployment, business cycles, and monetary/fiscal policies.

Business Statistics

Equips students with skills to collect, analyze, and interpret data using methods like probability, sampling, and hypothesis testing.

YEAR THREE

Mastering the Finance Specialization

The final year provides deep, specialized knowledge in the core disciplines of finance, preparing students for specific roles in the financial services industry.

Corporate Finance

Advanced study of a corporation's major financial decisions, including capital structure, dividend policy, and corporate valuation.

Investment Analysis & Portfolio Management

Equips students with skills to analyze securities, construct efficient portfolios, and evaluate investment performance using models like CAPM.

Financial Markets & Institutions

An in-depth study of the financial system's architecture, including money markets, capital markets, and the role of regulators.

International Finance

Addresses the complexities of operating in a global environment, focusing on foreign exchange markets and risk management.

Strategic Management

A capstone course on formulating and implementing business strategy, integrating knowledge from all previous subjects.

Finance Electives

Specialized courses like Financial Derivatives, Risk Management, and Income Tax Law allow students to tailor their education to specific career interests.

Disclaimer: The curriculum details provided are intended to represent a general BBA program structure. The specific subjects, their content, and their sequencing may vary significantly between universities and colleges based on their academic design and objectives. This information should be considered an illustrative guide and not a definitive standard.
