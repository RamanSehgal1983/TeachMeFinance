Introduction

Last Updated: August 23, 2025

Welcome to teachmefinance.online (the "Website"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with transparency and care. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of your information through the Website.

Our Website is a static, educational platform designed to provide information on financial course curriculums. We do not require user registration, and our goal is to collect as little personal information as possible. By using our Website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2

Information We Collect

We collect personal information from you only when you voluntarily provide it to us. The only point of collection on our Website is through our "Contact Us" form. The information we collect includes:

  • Name: To address you properly in our response.
  • Email Address: To send you a reply to your inquiry.
  • Message: The content of your query or feedback.

We do not use cookies for tracking or collecting personal data, nor do we use any other analytics or tracking technologies that would identify individual users.

3

How We Use Your Information

The personal information you provide through the "Contact Us" form will be used for the sole purpose of responding to your questions, comments, or inquiries. We do not use this information for any other purpose, such as marketing, creating user profiles, or selling to third parties.

Our website is static and does not have a back-end database to store personal information. Once your inquiry has been addressed, we will not retain your personal information.

4

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We are committed to maintaining your trust, and we want you to understand that our business is providing financial education, not selling data.

We may only disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights, prevent wrongdoing, or protect the safety of our users or the public.

5

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. While our data collection is minimal, we take reasonable administrative and technical measures to safeguard the information you provide via our "Contact Us" form. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6

Links to Other Websites

Our Website may contain links to other websites of interest. However, once you use these links to leave our site, we do not have control over that other website. Therefore, we cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information you provide whilst visiting such sites. You should exercise caution and review the privacy statement applicable to the website in question.

7

Children's Privacy

Our Website is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take steps to remove that information.

8

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
